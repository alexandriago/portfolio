Digiday Careers

UI / front-end dev

The Brief

Digiday needed a design for the career board that would be used by both job seekers and employers. Job seekers would be able to search and apply for jobs, and employers would be able to post jobs and search for candidates.

Digiday Careers Home

What I Did

As visual designer at Digiday, I designed the career board's user interface for two types of users: job seekers and employers. After creating a job seeker account, users can create a profile, upload their resumes, and save job listings. With an employer account, users can manage their job posts and search for candidates.
